# Design Document — Sean Behan Personal Homepage

## Project Description

Sean Behan's personal homepage is a static website that serves as a central platform for everything Sean has done and is passionate about. The site covers his professional career in computer science, his running, his academic experience, and personal interests like video games and movies. Any visitor whether a recruiter, a fellow runner, or a friend can find what's relevant to them. Each interest has its own dedicated subpage linked from the main homepage so visitors can go deeper into whatever they care about most. Additionally, the site serves as a visual representation for Sean's personal interests, giving him an interactive and personalized page specifically for his advancements within his life.

## User Personas

### Persona 1: Lily Williams
**Age:** 32
**Occupation:** Software Systems Engineer at Strava

**Goals:**
- Quickly assess Sean's technical background and project experience
- Get a sense of how Sean approaches and solves problems through his projects
- Find links to his GitHub and LinkedIn
- Get a sense of his academic coursework and co-op experience

**Frustrations:**
- Can't easily find his GitHub or professional links
- Academic and project experience is hard to navigate or not presented clearly
- Too much personal content makes it hard to find the professional stuff
- A resume alone doesn't reveal someone's true passion; wants to see actual projects to get a real sense of what Sean cares about

### Persona 2: Jake Miller
**Age:** 18
**Occupation:** Incoming Freshman at Northeastern University

**Goals:**
- Learn about Sean's running journey and progression
- Get a sense of what Northeastern Club Running offers and how it has helped Sean grow
- Find race times and milestones to understand what level of commitment the club requires

**Frustrations:**
- Can't find specific details about Sean's running progression or times
- No clear connection between the club and Sean's personal growth as a runner
- Wants to see a personal account not just a general overview of the club

### Persona 3: Chris Velma
**Age:** 21
**Occupation:** College Student

**Goals:**
- See what video games Sean has been playing recently for recommendations
- Find out what movies or shows Sean has watched and enjoyed
- Get a sense of Sean's taste to see if their interests align

**Frustrations:**
- Can't easily find recent activity or recommendations
- Content is buried under professional and running information
- No clear way to see what Sean is currently into

## User Stories
### Lily Williams (Recruiter)
- As a recruiter at Strava, I want to see a visual demo or snippet of Sean's projects in action so that I can quickly assess his capabilities and tech stack without having to clone and run each repo myself.
- As a recruiter at Strava, I want to see a categorized list of relevant coursework Sean has completed so that I can verify he has the fundamentals needed for a systems software engineering role.
- As a recruiter at Strava, I want to see that Sean has a personal passion for the fitness and running space so that I can gauge whether he would be genuinely motivated working at a company like Strava.

### Jake Miller (Incoming Freshman Runner)
- As an incoming freshman at Northeastern, I want to see Sean's race times and running progression over time so that I can understand what level of commitment and improvement is possible through Northeastern Club Running.
- As an incoming freshman at Northeastern, I want to read about Sean's personal experience with the club so that I can get a genuine first hand account rather than a generic club description.
- As an incoming freshman at Northeastern, I want to see photos and highlights from club events so that I can get a feel for the culture and community before I commit to joining.

### Chris Velma (Friend)
- As a friend of Sean's, I want to see a list of games Sean has been playing recently so that I can find something new to play based on his recommendations.
- As a friend of Sean's, I want to see what movies and shows Sean has watched and enjoyed so that I can find something to watch that aligns with his taste.
- As a friend of Sean's, I want to see Sean's ratings or thoughts on what he has watched and played so that I can get a sense of whether I would enjoy it too.
